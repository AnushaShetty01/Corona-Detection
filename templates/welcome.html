CORONA DETECTION USING Chest X-ray IMAGES

1. What is it? Covid-19 is a disease caused by a virus called SARA-CoV-2

2. Who can get it? Anyone can get sick with COVID-19, but older people and those with certain health conditions are
more at risk

3. How does it spread? The virus spreads through small liquid particles from an infected person’s mouth or nose when
they cough, sneeze, speak, sing or breathe.

4. How to prevent it? Stay at least 1 metre apart from others, wear a mask,wash your hands often, and get vaccinated
when it’s your turn.

5. What to do if you’re sick? If you feel unwell, stay home and self-isolate until you recover.

6. Stay informed: Follow advice from your healthcare provider and local health authorities.
